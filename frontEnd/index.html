Welcome to Hartebeesport Dam
Water Hyacinth Monitoring

A platform for tracking, analyzing, and mitigating water hyacinth spread.

Our platform collects vital environmental data such as temperature, pH, and salinity – offering a comprehensive view of hyacinth growth patterns. Join our community to monitor infestation levels, contribute data, and access innovative mitigation tools.
